How our love blossomed

Our Story

It all began with a simple hello. From strangers, we became friends, and soon, our hearts found a rhythm that danced to the same beat. Through life's ups and downs, we've shared countless moments of joy and laughter, growing stronger together with each passing day.

  • First Meet

    21 September 2023

    Son first saw Mariyanti at a cozy café one evening. She was sitting alone, reading a book, and her smile caught his eye. Nervously, he approached her, sparking a conversation about the book she was reading. Hours passed as they talked, laughed, and discovered they had a lot in common. That night, they both knew something special had begun.

  • On A Relationship

    20 November 2023

    As weeks turned into months, Son and Mariyanti grew closer. Their weekends were filled with adventures—hiking, trying new restaurants, and having deep conversations. Son admired her kindness and intelligence, while Mariyanti loved Son’s energy and passion for life. They became inseparable, supporting each other through life’s challenges and celebrating their joys together.

  • We are Engaged

    28 October 2024

    After years of growing together, Son knew Mariyanti was the one he wanted to spend his life with. On a special evening, he took her back to the café where they had their first date. As they reminisced about their journey, Son pulled out a ring and asked Mariyanti to marry him. With tears in her eyes and a beaming smile, she said yes, marking the beginning of their new chapter together.
